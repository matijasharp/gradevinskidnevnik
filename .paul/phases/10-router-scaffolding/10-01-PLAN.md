## Goal
Install react-router-dom, configure Vite for SPA history fallback, and scaffold the router skeleton (routeConfig, AppRouter, ProtectedRoute, GuestRoute) — without activating it.

## Purpose
Phase 10 builds the routing foundation that Phase 12 will activate. Creating the router scaffold now lets Phase 11 (AuthProvider) and Phase 12 (navigate() migration) build on a stable base. The governing constraint applies: zero behavior change — the router is scaffolded but NOT mounted in main.tsx or App.tsx.
