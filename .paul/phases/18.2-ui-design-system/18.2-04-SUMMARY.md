## Decisions Made

| Decision | Rationale | Impact |
|----------|-----------|--------|
| NavItem active = left border accent, not full bg | ROADMAP spec: "accent indicator for active item (left border or dot, not full bg)" | Cleaner visual; matches enterprise sidebar conventions |
| Sidebar w-60 (240px) not w-64 (256px) | ROADMAP specifies 240px expanded | Consistent with spec; outer padding updated to md:pl-60 |
| BrandPage /brand not in AppShell nav | Dev/design tool, not user-facing | No nav pollution; accessible at /brand to any authenticated user |
